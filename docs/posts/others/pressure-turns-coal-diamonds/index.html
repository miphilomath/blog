Pressure turns coal into diamond!

May 22, 2020 2-minute read

During my exam I study whole new subject in a day and I don’t know how, I complete whole book in a day, though it strains a lot later, felt too tired thereafter.

So I decide to study important subjects again, to understand their core properly and I feel I can study whole book in a day, I can do it in a week without straining my mind and body.

Okay! Lot of distraction for now. Pressure is the reason someone can do great stuff which others cannot.

My friend said, “Pressure turns coal into diamonds!".

Pressure can be internal as well as external which can help you achieve something you want to but you didn’t by now (maybe because you didn’t have enough inspiration to complete that stuff to pressurize yourself).

This concludes that we should do what we love to remain inspired and pressurized (not necessarily external).
