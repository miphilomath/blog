Elitebnc after being in service for more than 5 years, is shut in January 2020 and many ghosts who was relying on Elitebnc bouncer to exist, were being exorcised.

IRC all the way? (how to remain online on IRC everysecond)

Bouncers? I was on elitebnc for quite sometime, until they shut down

Why not quassel or IRCcloud? Maxking wrote a post on persistent irc setup and shared his reasons to use weechat over everything else. My reasons of using weechat are pretty much similar, so sharing the link to his blog post https://asynchronous.in/2017/03/15/Persistant-IRC-Setup/

Setup:

Docker or on host

SSL through openssl or let’s encrypt: https://pthree.org/2016/05/20/weechat-relay-with-lets-encrypt-certificates/

openssl req -x509 -nodes -newkey rsa:2048 -keyout relay.pem \
  -subj "/O=weechat/CN=europa-ssdnodes" \
  -config <(cat /etc/ssl/openssl.cnf <(printf "\n[v3_ca]\nsubjectAltName = @alternate_names\n[alternate_names]\nIP.1 = \${ENV::IP}")) \
  -days 365 -out relay.pem
