Prelogue!

Last Year i.e., 2019, I gifted myself a lot of books from amazon, the ones I have been recommened by others and I was planning to read soon. And, only an year after I got the time to pick the book, I was most excited to read. It’s not I didn’t read in the meanwhile, I bought more books and piled them over this one. This year, I am on a streak of reading atleast 1 book a month and i selected Quiet, the power of introverts for the month of May, which I picked after watching the Ted talk by Susan Cain, the author of this amazing book.

Also, this blog post will be my first ever review on any book I have read. Each time I read a book, I make few notes somewhere as go through the chapters, writing and noting down all the realisation I have and the lessons I learn. (Since most of knowledge about comes from reading, typical introvert I believe)
