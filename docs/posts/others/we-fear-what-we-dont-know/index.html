We fear what we don't know!

January 26, 2020 2-minute read
others • insights • life
others • learning

You might all know, that there was an era when people do shitty ceremonies to praise their gods like sacrificing and all. And you must be thinking, only a fool can think so. Then you are wrong, everyone including you and me, are fool enough to do same shitty things when it comes to thing that we don’t understand.

Confused?

Well, it’s not any theory, but one thing that I believe might be the reason.

Like all, humans when confronted a new technology, disease or anything else. The first thing they will feel fear, mainly because if something is foreign to them, we can’t just trust it unless we understand it.

What do you think, cavemen have reacted when fire was invented (may be mistakenly). They must have feared it, yet curious enough to give it a try, get themselves hurt by burning. And with time, they get along with its properties and used it for their benefits. That’s how use of fire must have evolved.

Similarly, like me, there are other people, who know new technology for encryption exists but never used it and don’t know why? Here’s the reason, you haven’t done your homework. Once we get along a technology, its functioning, working, concepts behind it and whether it’s useful to you or not, we can start to use. Or we can just take a risk of trying something and see what’s happen. (which I believe the other type of people do)
