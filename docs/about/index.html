Blog

This blog is still a work in progress. Over a period of time, many attempts were made to keep this running, with my learnings, findings and perspective but failed. Perfectionism can be blamed for this. I tried to launch separate blogs for my perspective, findings and a separate blog with more professional articles and tutorials.

It’s not yet decided, whether I want to keep separate blogs or not, but for now posts can be filtered using the categories and tags to help you reduce the noise. Until I have enough blog posts to create separate blog for them, all of my blog posts will be published on this website.

This blog will contain posts from various categories as a true learner never discriminates. When I learn something I find interesting, I will try to publish about them here. The blog posts will mostly cover these topics, computers, physics, life, languages, books and many DIY stuff. I will also try to include post on travel, photography and my used to be hobby, sketching.
