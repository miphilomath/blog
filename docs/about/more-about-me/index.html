Nice to see you here and thank you for showing interest to know me. One word to describe me would be “philomath”.

Yes, a philomath. A single word that describes me and my thirst for knowledge. Philomath originates from combining 2 greek words, “Philos and “math” that means, “lover of learning” or “knowledge seeker”.

I love to learn new tools, techniques, concepts and everything that is fascinating. I learn from everyone and everything and don’t mind a constructive criticism.

I love to simplify the sophistication in things around us, i.e. the world and computers, thus Physics, Mathematics and Computers fascinates me the most.

I like advocating open source communities and technologies along with data privacy tools. My daily job includes reading, writing, programming and learning.
