This blog post is the announcement of our upcoming podcast, Decompiled! But Why a podcast? Here is why.

Target audience for entire podcast

  • Budding Developers seeking to learn about new technologies and expoloring the industry trends for them.
  • Students to learn about Computers equipped with programming, an eye opening technology, which broadens their perspective towards things
  • Teachers and Mentors
  • Mostly Open Source Enthusiast, and communities

Why we are starting this podcast

  • Why not? We all want to learn about computers and their wonders, but still procastinating to learn them. Our podcast will let everyone, from absolute beginners to industry experts to improve their domain knowledge of technology. Our podcast, instead of dealing with any single technology, will deal with computer and technology in general, i.e., how computer technology changed our lives from hardware to Artificial Intelligence, From mainframe to SIRI [3]. What technologies are used in the industry and how do they revolved around, their importance and should anyone care about it or not.
